About

Beezig is a 5zig plugin that adds support for the Hive Minigames (Java) server.

Advanced Records

Adds advanced stats to the otherwise poor /records (/stats) command, including hidden stats, ratios and more.

Discord Rich Presence

Show the world what you're playing, with detailed info about the game.

Modules HUD

A collection of useful HUD modules that help you focus on important aspects of the game.
